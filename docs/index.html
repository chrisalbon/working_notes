Working notes on AI

This isn't a newsletter in the usual sense. No big insights. No deep dives. No tutorials. Just my working notes on AI.

I've been working in AI and data for a long time, and over the years, I've developed a habit: reading something —- a blog post, an academic paper, a book —- and jotting down a thought in the margins. Sometimes it's a question, sometimes a connection to something else I've read, sometimes a thought that I'm not sure I'll ever be able to answer. These notes are usually messy. They aren't conclusions, just artifacts of the thinking process.

If you're interested in seeing those notes, feel free to subscribe.
